## Hold points

Do not freeze panel geometry until the following are physically measured with calipers:

1. Powerpole panel mount body and retention features
2. USB-C input panel extension or trigger board
3. Master switch bezel and snap tabs
4. Voltage display body and retaining nut
5. Fuse block footprint and service-clearance envelope
